Mouse Suit Playbook

Simple rules that keep it safe, consistent, and professional.

Do’s
  • Handler stays close and guides the Mouse through crowds and obstacles
  • Handler refers to the teammate in suit as “Mouse”
  • Keep the experience family-friendly and upbeat
  • Pack everything back in the bag after the event
Don’ts
  • The Mouse does not speak
  • The Mouse does not remove its head in public
  • No eating or drinking while wearing the suit
Mouse Suit Items (quick inventory)
